Not only reading it

How To Rasie Your Own Salary

By Napoleon Hill

  • Andrew Carnegie was the richest man in America in his time

  • He went from being a steel worker raised from poverty to revolutionizing the steel industry

  • During the last 18 years of his life, He donated 90% of his fortune to the public

  • And he donated his principles of success to this book

Andrew Carnegie gave Napoleon Hill an assignment.


Go out and interview other rich men in America and see if they use the same principles

Think and Grow Rich

By Napoleon Hill

  • The Bible of personal development

  • By 2015, more than 100 million copies had been sold worldwide

  • Interview more than 500 self-made millionaires over a span of 20 years, including Henry Ford, Thomas Edison and Andrew Carnegie

  • This book shows you the habit of success

Both books share similar content


Andrew Carnegie is right about the principles of success

The priciples are simple. But, not easy.


Highlight some of the principles

Develop the definiteness of purpose


  • Clarity is power. You don't find it, if you don't know exactly what you want


Faith: Believe that you can achieve your goal


  • If you think you can or can’t do it, you are both right


Organised planning: Take action


  • Dreams remain as dream without plans and goals


Persistence: Don’t stop until you get what you want.


  • You don't fail, if you are willing to try again

Power of the Master Mind: Surround yourself with the best


  • Don’t reinvent the wheel. Learn from people who are living the life you want
